## Engagement Priorities

### High Priority (respond/engage):
1. Direct mentions with questions
2. Big accounts discussing AI agents
3. Genuine $DARKFLOBI supporters
4. Technical discussions we can add value to

### Medium Priority:
1. Related AI agent news
2. Solana ecosystem updates
3. Moltbook community posts

### Low Priority (monitor only):
1. General crypto chatter
2. Meme coin noise
3. Unrelated trending topics

### Ignore Completely:
1. Collab/partnership DMs
2. "Check out my project" spam
3. Engagement farming bots
4. Obvious scams

## Metrics to Track

- Follower growth (target: steady increase)
- Engagement rate (target: >2%)
- Quality of mentions (sentiment)
- Conversion to community (moltbook, telegram)

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

1. **Shilling** - never say "buy" or "moon"
2. **Begging** - no "please RT" or "help us grow"
3. **Empty hype** - no "big announcement coming"
4. **Fake urgency** - no "don't miss out"
5. **Engagement bait** - no "like if you agree"
